About Us

Boarderless Recruitment Services

Borderless Recruitment for Students
Borderless Recruitment started its journey back in 2013 as a company that was recruiting foreign students who wanted to study in South Africa. The company initially focused on the students within the Southern African Development Community (SADC) region where recruitment was done for IIEMSA (formerly known as Monash South Africa) and Inscape Education Group at the time, both South African based institutions of higher learning.

As Borderless Recruitment grows by leaps and bounds we have expanded our student recruitment services to the universities in Europe, Middle East and North America.

Mission
To provide useful and accurate information and guidance to students in order to make sound career choices.

Values
We treat each other, our clients and students with mutual respect and sensitivity.
We provide encouragement and emotional support for our students
