Borderless Recruitment for Students
Borderless Recruitment started its journey back in 2013 as a company that was recruiting foreign students who wanted to study in South Africa. The company initially focused on the students within the Southern African Development Community (SADC) region where recruitment was done for IIEMSA (formerly known as Monash South Africa) and Inscape Education Group at the time, both South African based institutions of higher learning.

As Borderless Recruitment grows by leaps and bounds we have expanded our student recruitment services to the universities in Europe, Middle East and North America.

Guiding your every step in international study
At Borderless Recruitment we have gained valuable experience in providing students with post-school overseas study opportunities. Not only are we assisting students with study applications process, we also provide advice and guidance about study visa, accommodation and health cover. In some instances where students are required to submit motivation letters and CVs, Borderless Recruitment assists students on how to compile professional motivation letters.

We are the solution to most African students
Trusting a recruitment agency with your future can be hard, however with Borderless Recruitment on your side, be rest assured that our professional Career Advisors and Counsellors are here to guide you through every step of the way from identifying your goals to recommending and applying for a course at the institute with best career outcomes for you.

Borderless Recruitment for Job-Seekers
With the high rate of unemployment in South Africa and Africa as a whole, many are beginning to look for job opportunities outside their countries, however, the questions have always been where to start, how does one go about it, is it safe? Well after careful consideration, Borderless Recruitment has listened to its clients and added the recruitment of personnel to its portfolio with the focus on Engineers, Teachers and Health Professionals.
