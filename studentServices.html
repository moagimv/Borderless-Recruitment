Recruitment of Students for Universities

As a recruitment agency for a number of universities locally and abroad, our core business is to recruit students for our partner universities.

Student Accommodation

Working together with our partner universities, we assist students to secure accommodation at the universitiesâ€™ residences or accredited private accommodation. Some accommodation especially at universities residences are fully furnished while privately owned ones may not.

Study Visa

Applying for study visa is one of the daunting process which differs from country to country. Borderless Recruitment provides information and guidance on application process, supporting documents and visa fees.

Health Cover

As part of study visa conditions, most countries require foreign students to have a health cover while studying abroad. Borderless Recruitment helps students find suitable health cover and insurance that is acceptable to the institution and the country.

Education Expo

As a drive to make students, parents and sponsors aware of the overseas study opportunities, we organize education expos where our partner universities come and meet with their potential students

Choosing the Right Course/Programme

Choosing to study in a foreign country can be intimidating for many, hence it is important to consult with the experts like us who will provide information on the possible courses based on your subjects, interests and results. Our professional staff will assist you with the development of a comprehensive plan to get you where you want to study. We advise students on choosing the course, where to study, admission requirements, how much will it cost and the right time to apply. We apply on behalf of our students or we guide them through the application process.

Pre-departure Counselling

To our students prepare for life in a new country, we offer pre-departure counseling for them which helps them to navigate personal and cultural growth opportunities while studying abroad. These sessions include information on what to pack, arrival in the new country, banking, cultural shock and other related information.

Flight Bookings (Optional)

We have received numerous requests from some of our students who in addition to facilitate the study application process, requested assistance with flight bookings to and for their study destinations.

Scholarships, Grants and Study Loans

It is generally believed that international education is expensive, hence most academic institutions offer scholarships or grants to ease the tuition burden on students.

Most universities that we recruit students for offer scholarship opportunities with terms and conditions, some are fully funded scholarships while others are partial scholarships or grants.

We provide guidance on how to apply for scholarships, in some instances assisting students with writing the motivation letters for scholarships applications. For South African students there are companies offering study loans which come with terms and conditions. We are able to facilitate the study loan application process provided the student has the university admission letter and proof that someone will be able to repay the loan while the student is studying (terms and conditions apply)

If a student comes from a country where English is not their first language, some universities require that such students should take the IELTS test to provide proof of English Language ability.

Borderless Recruitment facilitate the booking application of IELTS test for students irrespective of where they are in the world.
